# NDA and Evidence Sanitization Methodology

All work documented in this repository was performed in private environments
under non-disclosure agreements (NDA).

To protect organizational security:
- No screenshots from production environments are shared
- No tenant identifiers, device names, or internal URLs are included
- No policy exports, logs, or configuration files are published

Evidence is represented through written documentation to demonstrate
implementation approach, validation methodology, and security outcomes.

Detailed evidence artifacts may be made available upon request in accordance
with NDA and organizational approval.
